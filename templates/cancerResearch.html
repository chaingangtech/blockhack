About this Research 

“ At Beta Bionics, Inc., we are developing a bionic pancreas system called the iLet™, a revolutionary fully integrated and wearable bionic pancreas medical device platform that automatically and autonomously manages blood sugar levels in people with insulin-dependent type 1 diabetes (T1D) 24/7. The iLet promises to dramatically improve clinical outcomes and profoundly reduce the burden and cost of diabetes care.
